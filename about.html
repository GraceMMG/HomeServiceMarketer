Our Story

We Saw a Broken System — and Fixed It

In 2019, our founder spent two years managing marketing accounts for home service businesses at a digital agency. He watched skilled, hardworking contractors — HVAC technicians, roofers, plumbers, electricians — get charged $3,000–$5,000 a month for generic campaigns that barely worked.

The problem wasn't effort. It was the tools and templates being used. Generic small-business marketing doesn't work for the trades. The seasonality is different. The keywords are different. The customer buying cycle is different. The offers, the urgency, the trust signals — all different.

So we built our own playbook. Trade-specific campaigns, ad templates, email sequences, and systems built from the ground up for contractors. The results were dramatic — and the toolkits almost immediately paid for themselves.

HomeProMarketer.com was born from the belief that every hardworking contractor deserves access to professional-grade marketing — at a price that doesn't hurt.
