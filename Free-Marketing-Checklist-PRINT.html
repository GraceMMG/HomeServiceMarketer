Foundation & Visibility
1
Google Business Profile Claimed & Verified
Easy

Your free Google listing is live, verified, and fully filled out with your services, hours, and photos — so you show up in local map results when customers search.

2
Website Loads in Under 3 Seconds on Mobile
Medium

Over 70% of homeowners search on their phone — a slow website kills your leads before they even read your offer. Test yours at PageSpeed Insights for free.

3
Phone Number is Click-to-Call on Mobile
Easy

Your phone number on your website is a real tap-to-call link — not an image or plain text — so mobile visitors can reach you with one tap instead of copying it manually.

4
Google Ads Conversion Tracking Is Active
Advanced

You have Google Ads conversion tracking installed so you know exactly which keywords and ads are generating calls and form fills — not just clicks.

5
Facebook Pixel Installed on Your Website
Medium

The Facebook Pixel is installed on your site so you can retarget visitors, build lookalike audiences, and track conversions from your Facebook and Instagram ads.

Reviews, Content & Budget
6
At Least 25 Google Reviews (4.5★ or Higher)
Medium

You have 25 or more Google reviews averaging 4.5 stars — enough social proof that customers choose you over competitors with fewer or lower-rated reviews.

7
Posting to Google Business Profile Weekly
Easy

You post a photo, promotion, or update to your Google Business Profile at least once a week — keeping your listing active and improving your local search ranking.

8
Email Follow-Up Sequence for New Leads
Medium

Every new lead or website inquiry automatically receives a follow-up email sequence — at minimum a same-day response plus a 3-day follow-up — so no lead goes cold.

9
Before & After Photos in Ads and Social Media
Easy

You regularly use real before-and-after photos of your work in your Facebook ads, Google ads, and social posts — because nothing sells home services better than visual proof.

10
Monthly Ad Budget Allocated by Channel
Advanced

You have a defined monthly ad spend for each channel (Google, Facebook, LSA) with a target cost-per-lead — so you're investing intentionally, not guessing.

5–7 Getting There

8–10 Strong Foundation

You're ahead of most contractors. Now it's time to optimize and scale what's already working.
